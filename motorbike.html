Before you set off

Motorbike Tips & Tricks

🔑

CRITICAL: Always Power Off!

Turn the key to "OFF" every time you park or stop for photos. Leaving the ignition on can drain the battery completely — help is very hard to find in remote Cao Bang.

📡

CRITICAL: Smart Key Bikes

If your bike has a Smart Key, DO NOT press any buttons on the remote. Keep it in your pocket and use the ignition knob to start/stop the bike.

⛽

Top Up Fuel in the City

Always fill up before heading out. Fuel stations are scarce on remote mountain roads.

📵

Download Offline Maps

Download Google Maps offline before leaving. Mobile signal can be very weak in mountain valleys.

🌧️

Rain & Slippery Roads

If it rains, slow down significantly. Mountain roads become very slippery. Always carry your rain poncho — shops are rare on remote roads.

📯

Horn on Blind Curves

Gently beep before blind bends to alert oncoming traffic — a common and expected practice on mountain roads here.

🐃

Watch for Animals

Buffalo, chickens, and dogs often cross the road unexpectedly. Keep a moderate, steady pace — mountain curves are sharper than they look.

🔐

Lock the Handlebar

When parking, turn handlebars left and turn key to "LOCK" for better bike security.
