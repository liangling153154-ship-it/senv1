Good to know

We keep it real

🔇

Limited Soundproofing

Rooms have basic soundproofing. You may hear some ambient city noise or neighbouring rooms. Most guests sleep just fine — but light sleepers may want earplugs.

📺

No TV in Rooms

We don't have TVs. A projector with Netflix can be arranged in your room on request — subject to availability. Ask Sen when booking.

🔥

Heating Options

Cao Bang winters are cold. Most rooms have a heated mattress pad. The Pine Room has A/C with heating function. We'll make sure you're warm.

🏡

Homestay, Not Hotel

Rooms are simple, comfortable, and clean — not hotel-style luxury. The value is in the location, the views, and the local experience Sen provides.
